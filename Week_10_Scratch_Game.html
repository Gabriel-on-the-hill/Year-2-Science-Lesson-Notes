Game Rules:

  • A custom Ghost will keep disappearing and teleporting!
  • You have to CLICK it before it vanishes!
  • When caught, it will go POP! and change color!
  • Take turns with your friends to see who can catch it the most in 30 seconds!

Vector vs. Bitmap 🎨

Scratch has two ways to draw. Understanding the difference is your first Superpower!

Vector Mode

Shapes made of "Control Points". You can stretch them forever!

ZOOM: 100%

Bitmap Mode

Image made of "Pixels" (tiny squares). Like graph paper!

ZOOM: 100%
Convert to Bitmap
🚨 Rule: Always START in Vector mode so you can reshape!

1. "When the game starts, go to the Kitchen room."

3. "When I get clicked, tell the game I was caught!"
4. "If I touch the edge of the screen, bounce away."

Coding: The Ghost 👻

Our Ghost needs to be smart! In the Attic, we use GLIDE to make it harder!

1. Teleport (Kitchen)

when backdrop switches to [Kitchen]
forever
  hide
  wait (1.5) secs
  go to (random position)
  show
  wait (1.5) secs

2. Faster! (Bedroom)

when backdrop switches to [Bedroom]
forever
  hide
  wait (0.8) secs
  go to (random position)
  show
  wait (0.8) secs

3. Boss Mode (Attic)

when backdrop switches to [Attic]
forever
  hide
  wait (0.4) secs
  glide (0.5) secs to (random position)
  show
  wait (0.4) secs

4. Catch Me!

when this sprite clicked
  play sound [Pop] until done
  next costume
  hide
  broadcast [ghost-caught]
