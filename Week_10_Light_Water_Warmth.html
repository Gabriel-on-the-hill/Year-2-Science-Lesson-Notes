The Rule Breakers! 🌺

What is a Poppy Seed?

Poppy seeds are the tiny, crunchy blue-black seeds you sometimes see on bread! Because they are SO TINY, their "stored food lunchbox" is extremely small. If you bury them deep in the dark, they run out of food before reaching the surface. That's why they MUST sense sunlight to wake up!
