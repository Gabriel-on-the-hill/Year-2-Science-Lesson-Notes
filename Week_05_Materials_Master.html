🏆 Inventors Hall of Fame

These clever people solved BIG problems by inventing new materials!

👨‍🔬

John Dunlop

Problem: Bike rides were bumpy and uncomfortable!

Solution: Invented RUBBER tyres filled with air!

🎯 He made them for his son's tricycle!

🧥

Charles Macintosh

Problem: People got wet in the rain!

Solution: Created WATERPROOF fabric using rubber!

🎯 "Mac" raincoats are named after him!

🛣️

John McAdam

Problem: Roads were muddy and bumpy!

Solution: Made smooth roads from crushed rocks!

🎯 "Tarmac" comes from his name!
