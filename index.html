🧪 Year 2 Science & Computing

Interactive Lessons - Spring Term

🔍 Week 1: Shape Explorers
Investigate solid shapes by counting vertices, edges, and faces. Includes a "Shape Detective" activity and 3D vs 2D sorting.
🖐️ Week 2: Squash & Twist Lab
Virtual lab exploring forces on materials. Simulate squashing dough, twisting objects, and testing for malleability.
🦸‍♂️ Week 3: Hero Academy Lab
"Hero Training: Bend & Stretch Mission" - 20 interactive slides! Stretch-O-Meter, Gadget Lab, Real-World Uses, and Hero Exam.
🏅 Week 4: Material Olympics
"The Material Olympics" - Investigation-focused! Test materials for Bend, Stretch, Twist & Squash with data recording.
🕵️‍♂️ Week 4: Investigation Station
"The Investigation Station" - Fair Testing Lab! Help the 3 Little Pigs test bridge strength and roof waterproofing.
🏆 Week 5: Materials Master
Application Week! Spoon Challenge, Metal Detective, Shed Builder, Inventors Hall of Fame, and Final Quiz. 9 interactive slides!
🌱 Week 5: Garden Planner
"The Garden Project" - Builder Mode! Help Emmanuel build a shed using the correct materials for walls, windows, and roof.
🎓 Week 6: Mock KS1 Exam
A comprehensive year 2 assessment covering everything from Week 1 to 5. Natural/Man-made, Properties, Changing Shapes, Fair Tests, and Inventors.
🌱 Week 7: Seeds vs Bulbs
Interactive exploration of how plants start! "Kitchen Detective," Virtual Dissection, Matching Game, and Quiz. 8 interactive slides!
🔬 Week 8: Germination Station
Investigate how seeds grow! Featuring the "Radicle," seed peeling, growth time-lapse, Condition Sorting, and the Advanced 10-Question "Gold Standard" Quiz.
🌿 Week 9: Seedling Watch
Observe how seedlings grow! Featuring interactive growth timelines, anatomical labelling, health investigations, and the "Pedagogical Excellence" Quiz.
☀️ Week 10: Light, Water, Warmth
The "Big Three" growth factors! Featuring SVG animations for Water, Warmth, and Light mechanisms, interactive sorting, the Poppy seed experiment, and the 11-question Survival Quiz.
📝 Week 1 Quiz (Printable)
A simple, printable quiz to review Week 1 concepts about solid 3D shapes.
