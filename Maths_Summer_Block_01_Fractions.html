🔵 Finding a Half
A half means 2 equal parts. We shade 1 of the 2 parts.

Rectangle: ½ shaded
Triangle: ½ shaded
½ means 1 out of 2 equal parts. The bottom number (2) tells us how many parts. The top number (1) tells us how many are shaded.

Week 3
🔢 Unit vs Non-Unit Fractions
A unit fraction has a 1 on top (e.g. ½, ⅓, ¼).
A non-unit fraction has a number bigger than 1 on top (e.g. ²⁄₄, ³⁄₄).
¼ — Unit ✓
¾ — Non-Unit ✗
The top number (numerator) tells us how many parts are shaded.
If it's 1, it's a unit fraction. If it's more than 1, it's a non-unit fraction.
