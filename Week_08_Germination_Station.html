Our Secret Lab Words 🔑

To be an expert botanist, you need to know these secret codes!

Germination
The process by which a seed "wakes up" and bursts out of its shell! 💥
Sprout
When a seed or bulb first starts to put out green shoots. 🌱
Embryo
The tiny baby plant that is sleeping inside every single seed! 👶
Radicle
The very first root that grows out of a seed! It always points DOWN. ⬇️

🎉 Mission Accomplished, Botanist!

Here's the science: Seeds are amazing! They have a tiny embryo (baby plant) and a food store tucked inside. To wake up, they only need water 💧 to soften their coat and warmth 🌡️ to tell them it's spring! They don't even need soil 🪵 or sunlight ☀️ to start sprouting—all their energy is already packed inside!
