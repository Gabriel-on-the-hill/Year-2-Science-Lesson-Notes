🪄 Is 25 the same as 52?
They use the SAME digits… but are they the SAME number?
25
2 tens, 5 ones
vs
52
5 tens, 2 ones
The position of the digit changes its value!
The 2 in 25 means 20, but the 2 in 52 means just 2.
