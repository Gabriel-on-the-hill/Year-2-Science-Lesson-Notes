Plant Life Vocabulary 🔤

Click the cards to reveal the meanings of these important words!

Germination
When a seed starts to grow because it has water and warmth.
Sapling
A young, small tree. Larger than a seedling!
Seed Dispersal
When seeds move away from their mother plant to find space.
Nutrients
The "food" plants soak up from the soil to stay healthy.
Decompose
When old plants break down and turn back into soil.
