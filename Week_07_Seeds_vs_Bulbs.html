📚 Plant Words

Click the cards to flip them and learn the terms!

Seed 🌱
A baby plant with a tough coat and just a little bit of food. Needs waiting to grow.
Bulb 🧅
A baby plant packed with a huge food store wrapped in fleshy layers. Grows quickly in Spring!
Germinate 💧
When a plant "wakes up" and begins to grow after getting warmth and water.
Root ⬇️
The part of the plant that grows DOWN into the soil to drink water.
Shoot ⬆️
The green part of the plant that grows UP towards the sunlight.
